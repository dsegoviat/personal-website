About me

I am a fullstack developer in the last year of Computer Engineering, coursing the Software Engineering specialization. Developing, has been a way to express myself and making my ideas tangible. I love working other people, it makes me feel like I am part of something and allows me to learn from my partners and share my knowledge.

I love learning and try to keep myself updated about new technologies. This makes me feel like I am in continuous growth and do not plan to stop. I am a very communicative and resolutive person. Also, I try to do my work as perfect as it can be done by studying all the possible solutions and documenting the best one.

My job experience is related mostly with frontend technologies like Android, Angular, Ionic, ReactJS... But in 2016 I decided to focus my career in backend and DevOps solutions. My best language is Java but I have also worked with: C, C++, Python, Typescript and Kotlin.

In my free time I like spending some time with my loved ones and playing the guitar.
